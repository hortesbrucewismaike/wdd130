History

Our company began as a small group of adventure lovers who shared a passion for exploring rivers and nature. What started as weekend rafting trips among friends quickly grew into a professional service dedicated to providing safe and unforgettable experiences for everyone. Over the years, we have guided hundreds of visitors through exciting rapids while promoting respect for the environment and teamwork. Today, we are proud to be a trusted rafting company, committed to adventure, safety, and creating lasting memories for our clients.

Founded many years ago by a group of adventurous dreamers, our company started with just one raft and a big imagination. Through waves of challenge and splashes of success, we grew into a team of passionate guides who love the river and the thrill it brings. From small beginnings to great adventures, our story continues to flow like the river itself.
